Working and studying in Stuttgart, Germany

In 2016 I moved to live in Germany where I completed my Master of Science degree in integrated technologies in Architecture. It provided me with an unique opportunity to take part in Stuttgart University's research projects development process.

Research assistance projects:

  • - Buga Fiber Pavilion (Functional prototype development, model parametrization, fabrication process optimization, structural simulation.)
  • - Actuated multimaterials (Design and development of functional prototypes, development of data collecting system using RaspberryPi and NodeJS, research and fabrication of actuated 3D-printed materials.)

Thesis research project:

M.Sc. thesis titled “Fabrication strategies for precise application of reinforcement in 3D-printed concrete” was focused on expanding design space of 3D printed concrete through tailored reinforcement by means of robotic fabrication. Advised by Prof. A. Menges, Prof. Dr.-Ing. J. Knippers, T. Cheng, O. Bucklin
